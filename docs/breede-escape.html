Our Story

A place where
time slows down

Tucked into 12 hectares of fertile valley land along the Breede River, Breede Escape is where families, friends and colleagues come to reconnect — with each other and with nature. We're centrally situated between the Overberg, the Langeberg Meander and the Robertson Wine Valley.

We're a working farm producing vegetable seeds, with a herd of friendly Jersey cows you'll see grazing on the green pastures. There's no Wi-Fi here — just the sound of the river, old shady trees, and a campfire with your name on it.

Total relaxation, privacy, safety, and a close-to-nature experience. That's the Breede Escape promise.

What To Do

River life & adventures

🛶
Kayaking

Paddle the Breede River with kayaks provided at both camps. Slow float or gentle adventure — the choice is yours.

🏊
River Swimming

The Breede runs deep and cool alongside the camps. Private jetty access for a refreshing dip on warm Valley days.

🔥
Campfire & Braai

Every camp has its own campfire spot on the riverbank plus an indoor braai lapa for stories and slow cooking.

🏐
Volleyball

A volleyball court at the Private Camp keeps groups active. Combine with scenic farm walks to meet the Jersey cows.

🍷
Wine Routes

Robertson Wine Valley and Montagu are nearby. Spend a day on the acclaimed Langeberg Meander wine route.

🌄
Scenic Drives

Bonnievale, Swellendam, Robertson and Montagu all within easy reach. The Overberg offers breathtaking landscapes.

🐄
Farm Walks

Stroll through a working vegetable seed farm and meet the herd of friendly Jersey cows out on the green pastures.

🏃
Park Run

Saturday 08:00 Park Run at Weltevrede Wine Estate, followed by breakfast at the beloved Kapokbos coffee spot.

Finding Us

Getting here

We're situated 15km outside Bonnievale, centrally placed between the Overberg, the Langeberg Meander and the Robertson Wine Valley. Nearby towns:

  • Bonnievale15 km
  • Swellendam~40 km
  • Robertson~24 km
  • Montagu~45 km
From N1
Follow N1 through Worcester → turn RIGHT into Rabie Street (2.7 km) → follow signs to Robertson then Bonnievale → follow Breede Escape tourism signs.
From N2
Follow N2 past Caledon → take LEFT at Stormsvlei for Bonnievale → carry on 15 km → turn RIGHT for Gelukshoop/Drew → follow Breede Escape signs for 2.7 km.
Swellendam
N2 toward Ashton → turn RIGHT onto R60 (25 km) → turn LEFT for Bonnievale/Drew → cross the Breede River bridge → turn RIGHT at T-junction → follow signs.
