## Activities
- Kayaking: 🛶 — Paddle the Breede River with kayaks provided at both camps. Slow float or gentle adventure — the choice is yours.
- River Swimming: 🏊 — The Breede runs deep and cool alongside the camps. Private jetty access for a refreshing dip on warm Valley days.
- Campfire & Braai: 🔥 — Every camp has its own campfire spot on the riverbank plus an indoor braai lapa for stories and slow cooking.
- Volleyball: 🏐 — A volleyball court at the Private Camp keeps groups active. Combine with scenic farm walks to meet the Jersey cows.
- Wine Routes: 🍷 — Robertson Wine Valley and Montagu are nearby. Spend a day on the acclaimed Langeberg Meander wine route.
- Scenic Drives: 🌄 — Bonnievale, Swellendam, Robertson and Montagu all within easy reach. The Overberg offers breathtaking landscapes.
- Farm Walks: 🐄 — Stroll through a working vegetable seed farm and meet the herd of friendly Jersey cows out on the green pastures.
- Park Run: 🏃 — Saturday 08:00 Park Run at Weltevrede Wine Estate, followed by breakfast at the beloved Kapokbos coffee spot.
